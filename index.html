Henrik Lundqvist

One of the greatest goalies to ever play for the New York Rangers

  • 06/24/2000 - Selected in the 7th round, 205th overall by the New York Rangers
  • 10/08/2005 - NHL debut vs New Jersey Devils, losing 3-2 in overtime
  • 10/13/2005 - First career NHL win vs New Jersey Devils, winning 4-1
  • 10/17/2005 - First career NHL shutout vs Florida Panthers, winning 4-0, becoming the first Rangers goalie to record a shut out since John Vanbiesbrouck on Jan 2, 1985
  • 04/18/2006 - Finished the season with 30 wins, breaking the Rangers rookie goaltending record of 29 wins set by Jim Henry in 1942 and Johnny Bower in 1954
  • 06/23/2006 - Named to NHL All Star all rookie team
